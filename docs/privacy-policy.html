Privacy Policy — ImaginAI

At ImaginAI, we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how it’s used, and your rights regarding that data. It applies to our website, application, and related AI image generation services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide, personalize, and improve your experience with ImaginAI:

  • Account details: name, email address, and other profile information you provide.
  • Usage data: prompts, preferences, generated images, and technical logs that help us improve the AI engine.
  • Device & analytics data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and session duration for performance monitoring.
  • Cookies & local storage: to remember preferences, manage sessions, and analyze site activity (with consent where required).

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and enhance ImaginAI’s services and user experience.
  • To ensure system security, prevent fraud, and detect misuse.
  • To communicate service updates, respond to requests, and offer support.
  • To perform analytics and product research aimed at improving AI models.

3. Legal Basis

We process data based on (i) contract necessity for providing the service, (ii) legitimate interests in improving ImaginAI, (iii) user consent for optional data like cookies or marketing, and (iv) legal compliance.

4. Data Sharing

We never sell user data. Information may be shared only with trusted partners who assist in hosting, analytics, payment processing, or communications — always under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Retention

Personal data is stored only as long as necessary to operate our services or as required by law. Generated images and account data can be deleted upon user request.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information. You may also withdraw consent or request data export. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@imaginai.app.

7. Cookies

ImaginAI uses essential cookies for core functionality and analytics cookies to improve performance. You can adjust or revoke cookie preferences anytime in your browser settings.

8. Security

We apply industry-standard encryption and infrastructure protections. While no system is entirely immune, we continually work to enhance data security.

9. Policy Updates

This Policy may be updated periodically. The “Last updated” date indicates the most recent revision. Significant changes will be communicated through the platform.

10. Contact Us

For any questions or privacy-related concerns, please contact us at hello@imaginai.app.
